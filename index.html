Hi! Thank you for checking out my website! My name is Sakura, and I’m a 3rd year English major at UC Berkeley, double minoring in History and Creative Writing. I’ve worked for the Daily Californian for three years, covering everything from student government, to the UC Regents, to Berkeley city politics and elections, to the city’s homelessness crisis. I also love writing creatively, and have taken multiple classes in short fiction and poetry writing. On a less serious note, comic writing has been a hobby of mine for a few years — writing is my passion, regardless of medium.
Some random certifications I have are my first-degree black belt, my advanced open water scuba diving certification from the Professional Association of Diving Instructors and my American Red Cross Lifeguarding/First Aid/AED/CPR certificate.
If you want to see more about my writing, please feel free to read some clips and comics, check out my Portfolios page! If you’ve seen enough and want to get in contact, peep my Contact page! And, if you want to hear what others have said about me and want a good laugh, glance over my Reviews page! Thank you!
