Seasonal Reincarnation
Spring is born colorfully
Full of precious buds, which hide small beauties
Expectant with infant flowers, and
Anticipating groups prod branches
In wait of the tree’s pride and joy
The sun lays across the fertile land
Which leads time, forward to back
Into a cycle of life we can easily track
And the tree’s bare branches form ragged claws
The vibrant leaves and flowers long gone
And people don’t stop, rushing past in tight groups
Skeleton hands reach in various directions
Grappling with the cold wind, which whips between the limbs
A dead tree in winter’s coffin
Like a year, spring turns to winter, and winter turns backwards to spring.
